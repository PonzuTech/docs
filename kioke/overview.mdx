## What is a Kioke?

Traditional ponzu ferments in cedarwood barrels called a Kioke. Koji is the brewing culture.

In the Ponzu protocol, **Kioke Cards are ERC-721 NFTs** used for curation across the entire ecosystem. They help shape the culture and signal conviction. Kioke holders select which projects receive their backing and earn bonuses from tokens they endorse. Every token launch earns a voting credit called a Koji, and they are spent quadratically. 


We call it Quadratic Conviction Token Voting (or Koji Voting for short)


---

## How Kioke Cards Work

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Supply | 300 Genesis Kioke (fixed, no future minting) |
| Standard | ERC-721 NFT on Ethereum |
| Transferable | After sale. Koji balance, and active endorsements all transfer with the NFT |
| Rewards | Two streams: Crafting + Fermenting  |

---

## Crafting Rewards

**1% of all presale ETH** across every project launched on Ponzu flows to Kioke Card holders. Split equally among minted Kioke. 

If a project raises 100 ETH, 1 ETH enters the Kioke pool. 

You don't choose which projects. You don't stake anything. Hold a barrel, earn from every launch.

But you can only claim after you vote. If you spend half your Koji, you still accumulate your full share, but you can only claim half.

---

## Fermenting Rewards 

**5% of each project's Distributor rewards** (both tokens and ETH) go to the Kioke pool. But these rewards don't split equally across all 300 barrels. Kioke can earn boosted rewards for that token by spending Koji credits.

Your share of the 5% Kioke allocation for any project = your Koji burned on that project / total Koji burned on that project.


---

## Two Operating Modes

### Passive Holder
Buy a barrel. Earn crafting fees from every launch. Accumulate Koji over time. No decisions required. Burn Koji when you want to claim ETH.

### Active Curator
Burn Koji on projects you believe will generate sustained trading volume. Earn fermenting rewards from those specific projects. Your choices become your track record, on-chain and verifiable. 

Active curators with good judgment earn significantly more than passive holders. Your Koji is rate limited like every other Kioke, so curation becomes key.
