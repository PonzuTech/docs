## The Genesis Sale

300 Genesis Kioke barrels. Each is an ERC-721 NFT that earns revenue from every project launched on Ponzu through two streams: passive crafting fees (1% of all presale ETH) and active fermenting rewards (5% of endorsed project Distributor output).

The first barrel holders shape what gets launched on Ponzu. Your Koji endorsements are the signal layer.

## Pricing

Linear pricing. Each sale increases the price by a fixed increment.

**Starting price:** 0.1 ETH

No countdown timer. No Dutch auction. No hidden allocation.

## What Happens If the Sale Doesn't Fill

All 300 barrels exist in the contract. Unsold barrels remain available at their price point on the curve. No deadline. No minimum fill threshold.

If 150 sell and 150 remain, the protocol operates with 150 active barrels. Crafting revenue still distributes across all 300, but unsold barrels accumulate unclaimed revenue and Koji that future buyers inherit when they purchase.

Buy barrel #200 after 100 projects have launched, you get 100 accumulated Koji with the barrel plus backdated crafting revenue that accumulated while the barrel was unsold. Late buyers pay a higher price but inherit accrued value.
