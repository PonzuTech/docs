### Genesis Kioke

300 Genesis Kioke sold to celebrate the launch of Ponzu on Ethereum. 
The first barrel holders shape the future of Ponzu. 


### Linear pricing. 
Each sale increases the price by a fixed increment.

**Starting price:** 0.1 ETH
**Price increment:** 0.002 ETH per barrel sold

```
Barrel #1:   0.10 ETH
Barrel #50:  0.20 ETH
Barrel #100: 0.30 ETH
Barrel #150: 0.40 ETH
Barrel #200: 0.50 ETH
Barrel #250: 0.60 ETH
Barrel #300: 0.70 ETH
```

No countdown. No Dutch auction. Linear pricing.



### Kioke Claim

Tokens sent to the KiokeClaim on Ethereum are able to be claimed by Kioke Card holders, for ETH sent to the KiokeClaim, you can claim multiple times, but tokens you can only claim once.

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Crafting revenue** | 1% of presale ETH raised, split among barrels |
| **Koji accumulation** | 1 credit per barrel per factory launch, used for boosting |
| **Fermenting revenue** | 5% of Mainnet project's Distributor tokens (boosted with Koji) |
