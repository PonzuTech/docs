## The 3-Sentence Version

**The problem:** Token launches are built for extraction. Insiders get cheap tokens and dump on the community. Bots front-run every launch. Projects die before they ship anything.

**What Ponzu does:** Token factory with diamond-hand vesting. Claim your tokens early and you forfeit the unvested portion to everyone who waited. Refund before launch and get 90% of your ETH back. The 10% penalty goes to those who stayed. All rewards come from real fees, not printing new tokens.

**Why it matters:** You can fake followers, volume, and charts. You can't fake waiting. Ponzu makes patience the most profitable strategy, enforced by immutable contracts.

## The System

One transaction deploys eight contracts that wire together into a complete token ecosystem:

| Contract | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **Token** | ERC-20 with 1,000,000 fixed supply. No mint function. |
| **Presale** | Handles purchases, refunds, vesting, claims. 690,000 tokens (69%). |
| **Launcher** | Creates DEX liquidity at graduation. 310,000 tokens (31%). |
| **Distributor** | Routes all fees dynamically between presale holders, farmers, project, and Kioke. |
| **Farm** | LP staking for non-inflationary rewards. |
| **Ponzu Bottle** | ERC-721 NFT representing a presale position. Transferable. |
| **Liquidity Card** | ERC-721 NFT representing a farm staking position. Transferable. |
| **Project** | Registry and access control coordinator. |

After deployment, all parameters are fixed. No upgrade proxies. No admin keys. No migration steps. The system runs itself.

## Verify It Yourself

Every claim above is on-chain. Here's how to check:

| Claim | How to verify |
|-------|---------------|
| Fixed 1M supply | Call `totalSupply()` on the Token contract. Returns `1000000000000000000000000` (1M with 18 decimals). |
| No mint function | Read the Token contract source on Etherscan. No `mint()` function exists. |
| 69/31 split | Check `balanceOf(presaleAddress)` and `balanceOf(launcherAddress)` on the Token contract at deployment. |
| No admin keys | No `owner`, `admin`, or `onlyOwner` modifiers in any of the eight contracts after initialization. |
| LP permanently locked | The Presale contract holds LP tokens. No `withdraw`, `removeLiquidity`, or `transfer` function exists for them. |
| Fee splits | Read the Distributor contract. Hardcoded percentages, no setter functions. |

Factory contract (PonzuRecipe): [`0x1155484c5fE614538d83c444f9a6dB662E6a7153`](https://etherscan.io/address/0x1155484c5fE614538d83c444f9a6dB662E6a7153)

Every project deployed through PonzuRecipe emits a `PonzuCrafted` event with all eight contract addresses. Pull the event, read each contract, confirm the claims yourself.

## Worst Case Scenarios

Before you ape, know your downside:

| Scenario | What happens | Your max loss |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Project team is bad | Refund before launch | 10% token penalty + 4% fee already paid |
| Token dumps after launch | Claim Day 1, sell immediately | 90% of your token allocation (forfeited to community) |
| You need capital during vesting | Sell your Ponzu Bottle NFT on secondary | Depends on secondary market price, but position is liquid |
| Project never fills presale | Refund anytime | 10% token penalty + 4% fee |

The 90% refund before launch is the floor. After launch, diamond-hand vesting means early exits are expensive. Know the tradeoff going in.
