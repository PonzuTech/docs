## How Koji Works

Every time a token launches on Ponzu, each Kioke Card receives **10 Koji**. Koji is non-transferable between barrels, can't be bought, and can't be traded. You accumulate it by holding a Kioke through launches, and burn it on projects you endorse.

By default, **1 Koji is automatically allocated to every project** so you never miss out on rewards. An additional 10 accumulate in your barrel for voting on projects.


---

## Quadratic Voting

Koji is spent quadratically. The more you burn on a single project, the less each additional Koji is worth.

| Koji Burned | Bonus Credits |
|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | 1 |
| 4 | 2 |
| 9 | 3 |
| 25 | 5 |
| 100 | 10 |

Credits = √(Koji burned) + 1. 

Your share of a project's 5% Kioke rewards = your credits / total credits from all barrels on that project.

---

## What You Earn

When you endorse a project, you earn a share of that project's **5% Kioke allocation** from the Distributor. Rewards are paid in project tokens and ETH.

Back a project that sustains volume, your endorsement keeps paying. Back one that dies, your Koji is burned with nothing to show.

Early endorsement matters. First to endorse a winner, your share is large. The more Koji you stack on a token, the larger your share for that token.
