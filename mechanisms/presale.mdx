## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Presale">
    Linear pricing. Price starts at a tenth of your target and rises with each buy. Each position is a transferable Ponzu Bottle NFT. 690,000 tokens available (69% of supply). 90% ETH refund before launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Presale fills, anyone triggers launch. 310,000 tokens + raised ETH become permanent liquidity on PonzuSwap. LP tokens sit in a contract with no withdrawal function. The dev is dead the moment that button is pressed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diamond-Hand Vesting">
    One claim. That's it. Claim early, leave the rest for the patient. Wait the full vest, get your allocation plus a pro-rata bonus from everyone who left early. No one is locked in. Everyone makes their choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Farming">
    Stake LP, earn trading fees and redistributed tokens. Fixed 1,000,000 supply. No mint function. Every reward came from real economic activity. Impatient capital subsidizes the patient. Same principle, extended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linear Pricing

The founder sets a target raise. Price starts at one-tenth of the final price and increases at a constant rate as tokens sell.

```
startPrice = endPrice / 10
currentPrice = startPrice + (endPrice - startPrice) * (tokensSold / 690,000)
```

**Worked example:** 10 ETH target raise.

| Tokens Sold | Price per Token | Cumulative ETH Spent |
|-------------|----------------|---------------------|
| 0 (first buyer) | ~0.0000014 ETH | 0 ETH |
| 172,500 (25%) | ~0.0000050 ETH | ~0.55 ETH |
| 345,000 (50%) | ~0.0000086 ETH | ~2.20 ETH |
| 517,500 (75%) | ~0.0000122 ETH | ~4.95 ETH |
| 690,000 (100%) | ~0.0000158 ETH | ~10 ETH |

First buyer pays 10x less than the last buyer. Early conviction gets a better price.

## Refunds

Before launch, any buyer can call `refund()` to exit.

| Component | What Happens |
|-----------|-------------|
| ETH returned | 90% of your proportional share of the presale pool |
| Token penalty | 10% of your token allocation stays in the pool |
| Purchase fee | The 4% from your original buy was already distributed. Gone. |

**Round-trip math:** Send 1 ETH. 0.04 ETH (4%) distributed as fees. 0.96 ETH enters the pool. You refund. You get 90% of 0.96 = 0.864 ETH back. Total cost: 0.136 ETH (13.6%).

The 10% token penalty redistributes pro-rata to remaining holders. Bob refunds his 10,000 tokens, 1,000 stay in the pool. Alice's allocation grows from 10,000 to 11,000. The weak hands subsidize the diamond hands.

**Partial refunds** supported. Refund some tokens, keep the rest. Full refund burns the Ponzu Bottle NFT.

**On-chain:** `getPosition(tokenId)` on the Ponzu Bottle contract shows any position's current allocation and ETH contributed.

## Presale Completion

The presale completes when all 690,000 tokens are sold. No time limit. Projects can sell out in hours or months. Ship features while the presale fills.

Once sold out, anyone calls `triggerLaunch()`. No admin action required. Permissionless from this point forward.

**On-chain:** `tokensSold()` and `currentPrice()` are public view functions anyone can read.
