## How It Works

Traditional vesting unlocks gradually — claim a little today, a little tomorrow, sell continuously. Predictable, constant sell pressure. Cliffs only delay the inevitable.

Diamond-hand vesting flips this. Tokens vest linearly, but you get one claim. Pick your moment. You receive the vested portion. Everything unvested goes to the Distributor, which routes it to remaining presale holders, farm stakers, and the project treasury. Your Ponzu Bottle NFT is burned.

```
vestedAmount = (totalAllocation * elapsedTime) / vestingDuration
```

One claim forces a decision. No drip-selling. No constant sell pressure trickling into the market.

| Claim Day (10-day vest) | You Receive | Forfeited to Community |
|-------------------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Day 0 | 0% | 100% |
| Day 1 | 10% | 90% |
| Day 3 | 30% | 70% |
| Day 5 | 50% | 50% |
| Day 7 | 70% | 30% |
| Day 10+ | 100% + pro-rata share of all forfeitures | 0% |

**On-chain:** `getVestedAmount(tokenId)` to check vesting. `claim(tokenId)` to execute your one-time claim.

## The Forfeit Bonus

Holders who wait the full vesting period receive their original allocation plus a pro-rata share of everything forfeited by early claimers.

**Worked example:**

- Total presale: 690,000 tokens across 100 buyers
- You bought 10,000 tokens
- 60 buyers claim on Day 1, each receiving 10% and forfeiting 90%
- Those 60 held ~414,000 tokens combined. They forfeited ~372,600 tokens
- Forfeited tokens go to the Distributor:

| Recipient | Share of Forfeitures |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Remaining presale holders | 40% (~149,040 tokens) |
| Farm stakers | 40% (~149,040 tokens) |
| Project treasury | 15% (~55,890 tokens) |
| Kioke Card holders | 5% (~18,630 tokens) |

Your share of the presale holder portion: 10,000 / ~276,000 remaining presale tokens = ~3.6%. You receive ~5,366 bonus tokens on top of your 10,000.

**Note:** As more presale holders claim and exit, the presale holder share (40%) shifts toward 0% and the farm staker share (40%) shifts toward 80%. The Distributor rebalances dynamically. See the [Distributor page](/mechanisms/distributor) for the full allocation logic.

## Exit Early, But Leave the Rest

You can leave any time. The cost of leaving decreases with patience.

If the token has pumped 20x since presale, claiming 10% on Day 1 is still a 2x return in a single day. Nobody is punished. Impatient capital exits, leaving behind tokens for the patient. By the end of the vesting period, the remaining holders are those who chose to stay. You can't rush fermentation.

## ETH Rewards (Separate from Token Claim)

While vesting, Ponzu Bottle holders accumulate ETH rewards from the Distributor. These come from the 1% swap fee on every PonzuSwap trade.

Call `claimETH(tokenId)` whenever you want. This does not burn your Ponzu Bottle. This does not count as your one-time token claim.

After vesting ends, if you haven't claimed tokens, your Ponzu Bottle stays active. You keep earning ETH from swap fees indefinitely. No deadline. The smart contracts handle the economics.

**Distributor ETH split for presale holders:** 20% of all Distributor ETH goes to presale holders (shifting toward 0% as holders claim and exit). See the [Distributor page](/mechanisms/distributor) for exact allocation formulas.

## The Game Theory

Exit early, leave the rest.

Day 1: Keep 10%, forfeit 90%.
Day 5: Keep 50%, forfeit 50%.
Day 10: Keep 100% + your share of every forfeiture before you.

A trader who claims 20,000 tokens on Day 1 leaves 180,000 for the holders who stayed. The patient holder stays because waiting is the better move. People self-select into their natural position. Conviction over time.
