## Genesis Kioke

300 Genesis Kioke sold to celebrate the launch of Ponzu. 
The first barrel holders shape the future of Ponzu. 

---

## Pricing

Linear pricing. Each sale increases the price by a fixed increment.

**Starting price:** 0.1 ETH
**Price increment:** 0.002 ETH per barrel sold

```
Barrel #1:   0.10 ETH
Barrel #50:  0.20 ETH
Barrel #100: 0.30 ETH
Barrel #150: 0.40 ETH
Barrel #200: 0.50 ETH
Barrel #250: 0.60 ETH
Barrel #300: 0.70 ETH
```

No countdown. No Dutch auction. Linear pricing.


---

## What You Get

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Crafting revenue** | 1% of all presale ETH raised, split among barrels |
| **Koji accumulation** | 1 credit per barrel per factory launch, used for boosting |
| **Fermenting revenue** | 5% of project's Distributor tokens (boosted with Koji) |


---

## What Happens If the Sale Doesn't Fill

All 300 barrels exist in the contract. Unsold barrels remain available at their price point on the curve. No deadline. No minimum fill threshold.

If 150 sell and 150 remain, the protocol operates with 150 active barrels. Crafting revenue still distributes across all 300, but unclaimed revenue from unsold barrels accumulates in the contract.

Buy barrel #200 after 100 projects have launched, you start with 0 Koji. Late buyers pay a higher price and begin earning Koji from that point forward. The earlier you buy, the more launches you Koji you accumulate.
