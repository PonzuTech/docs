## Overview

The Distributor splits all protocol revenue dynamically based on claim progress. As presale participants claim their tokens, the allocation shifts automatically. No governance votes. No admin keys. No one can change the formula after deployment.

---

## Income Sources

| Source | Type | When |
|--------|------|------|
| Unvested tokens from early claimers | Project tokens | During vesting period |
| Refund penalties (10% of tokens) | Project tokens | Before launch |
| PonzuSwap trading fees (1%) | WETH | Ongoing after launch |

LP tokens from initial liquidity are held permanently by the Presale contract. No withdrawal function exists. That's what makes liquidity unruggable.

---

## Token Distribution

When the Distributor receives project tokens (from early claims and refund penalties), it splits them across four recipients:

| Recipient | Initial (0% claimed) | Mid (50% claimed) | Final (100% claimed) |
|-----------|---------------------|--------------------|---------------------|
| Presale holders | 40% | 20% | 0% |
| Farm stakers | 40% | 60% | 80% |
| Project treasury | 15% | 15% | 15% |
| Kioke holders | 5% | 5% | 5% |

**The shift formula:**
```
presaleShare = 40% x (1 - totalClaimed / totalClaimableAmount)
farmShare    = 40% + (40% x totalClaimed / totalClaimableAmount)
```

`totalClaimableAmount` is the actual number of tokens sold during presale (accounting for refund adjustments). As presale participants claim, their allocation decreases and the farm's increases. When the last person claims, the presale share hits 0% and the farm receives 80%.

**Project treasury** = the wallet of the project creator who deployed the token.

**Kioke holders** = holders of Kioke Card NFTs who have endorsed this specific project with Koji credits.

## ETH Distribution

When the Distributor receives WETH from swap fees, a fifth recipient enters the split: the Ponzu protocol itself.

| Recipient | Initial (0% claimed) | Mid (50% claimed) | Final (100% claimed) |
|-----------|---------------------|--------------------|---------------------|
| Ponzu protocol | 40% | 40% | 40% |
| Presale holders | 20% | 10% | 0% |
| Farm stakers | 20% | 30% | 40% |
| Project treasury | 15% | 15% | 15% |
| Kioke holders | 5% | 5% | 5% |

The dynamic presale-to-farm shift operates on a smaller range (20% instead of 40%) because the protocol takes its 40% first.

**ETH shift formula:**
```
presaleShare = 20% x (1 - totalClaimed / totalClaimableAmount)
farmShare    = 20% + (20% x totalClaimed / totalClaimableAmount)
```

## Worked Example

10 ETH in swap fees when 50% of presale tokens have been claimed:

| Recipient | Share | Amount |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Ponzu protocol | 40% | 4.0 ETH |
| Presale holders | 10% | 1.0 ETH |
| Farm stakers | 30% | 3.0 ETH |
| Project treasury | 15% | 1.5 ETH |
| Kioke holders | 5% | 0.5 ETH |

You held 5% of the presale and haven't claimed your tokens yet. Your ETH reward from this batch: 5% of 1.0 ETH = 0.05 ETH. Claimable immediately. Repeatable every time new fees accumulate.

Now assume 5,000 tokens were forfeited by early claimers during that same period:

| Recipient | Share | Amount |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| Presale holders | 20% | 1,000 tokens |
| Farm stakers | 60% | 3,000 tokens |
| Project treasury | 15% | 750 tokens |
| Kioke holders | 5% | 250 tokens |

## Lifetime ETH Rewards

Even after vesting ends, Ponzu Bottle holders who haven't claimed their tokens keep earning ETH from the Distributor. Pro-rata based on your share of the presale:

```
yourETHReward = (yourPurchases / totalPurchases) x ethSentToPresale
```

Claim ETH anytime by calling `claimETH(tokenId)`. You receive all accumulated ETH since your last claim. No minimum threshold. Repeatable.

Once you claim your tokens (burning your Ponzu Bottle), ETH rewards stop. The tradeoff: claim tokens early and lose future ETH income, or hold your Ponzu Bottle and keep collecting.

Conviction, extended.

## Permissionless Operation

No admin functions. Split ratios are calculated algorithmically from on-chain state. Anyone can trigger distribution by calling `distribute(token)` or `distributeETH()`. No one can change the formula or redirect funds. No proxy pattern. No upgrade mechanism. The bytecode is final.

**Griefing?** Calling `distribute()` with dust amounts costs the caller gas but doesn't affect other users. Your share doesn't change regardless of when distribution is called.

**Random tokens?** Sending a random ERC20 and calling `distribute(randomToken)` splits that token by current ratios. No harm to core flows. WETH must go through `distributeETH()`.

## On-Chain Verification

| Function | Returns |
|----------|---------|
| `getCurrentAllocations()` | Current basis point split for all recipients |
| `presaleAllocationBP()` | Current presale share in basis points |
| `farmAllocationBP()` | Current farm share in basis points |
| `getBalance(token)` | Undistributed token balance |
| `getETHBalance()` | Undistributed ETH balance |
