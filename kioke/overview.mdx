## What is a Kioke Card?

Kioke are the cedarwood barrels used for fermenting soybeans into traditional ponzu sauce. Some have been in continuous use for over a century. The fermentation culture Koji lives in the wood and enriches the flavor.

In the Ponzu protocol, **Kioke Cards are ERC-721 NFTs** that function as curation seats across the entire ecosystem. The factory is permissionless. Anyone can launch tokens. Kioke holders curate which projects receive their backing and earn from the ones they endorse.

300 total. Two revenue streams. Full transferability, including accumulated Koji and active endorsements.

## Revenue Stream 1: Crafting (Passive)

**1% of all presale ETH** across every project launched on Ponzu flows to Kioke Card holders. Split equally among all 300 barrels.

If a project raises 100 ETH, 1 ETH enters the Kioke pool. Your barrel's share: 1/300th, claimable anytime.

You don't choose which projects. You don't stake anything. Hold a barrel, earn from every launch.

### Crafting Revenue Model

| Annual Platform Presale Volume | Kioke Pool (1%) | Per Barrel (1/300th) |
|-------------------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| 1,000 ETH | 10 ETH | 0.033 ETH |
| 5,000 ETH | 50 ETH | 0.167 ETH |
| 10,000 ETH | 100 ETH | 0.333 ETH |
| 50,000 ETH | 500 ETH | 1.667 ETH |

Crafting revenue scales linearly with platform volume. No ceiling. No dilution. Fixed 300 barrels, forever.

## Revenue Stream 2: Fermenting (Active)

**5% of each project's Distributor rewards** (both tokens and ETH) go to the Kioke pool. But these rewards don't split equally across all 300 barrels. They go specifically to barrels that have **endorsed** that project using Koji credits.

The Distributor collects from three sources:
1. **Unvested tokens** forfeited by early claimers during diamond-hand vesting
2. **Swap fees** (1% on all PonzuSwap trades)
3. **Refund penalties** (10% token penalty from pre-launch refunds)

Your share of the 5% Kioke allocation for any given project = your Koji burned on that project / total Koji burned on that project.

### Fermenting Revenue Example

Project raises 100 ETH. After launch, it generates 500 ETH in monthly trading volume.

- Swap fees: 5 ETH/month (1% of volume)
- Kioke share of Distributor: 5% of swap fees + forfeited tokens
- Your endorsement: 10 Koji burned on this project out of 50 total = 20% of the Kioke allocation

Your fermenting earnings from this one project: ~0.05 ETH/month in fees + a proportional share of forfeited project tokens.

Multiply across every project you endorse. Successful endorsements compound.

## Two Operating Modes

### Passive Holder
Buy a barrel. Earn crafting fees from every launch. Accumulate Koji over time. No decisions required.

This is the baseline. Every barrel earns the same crafting revenue regardless of whether you endorse anything.

### Active Curator
Burn Koji on projects you believe will generate sustained trading volume. Earn fermenting rewards from those specific projects. Build a verifiable on-chain track record of your conviction.

Active curators with good judgment earn significantly more than passive holders. The fermenting stream has no cap and scales with the projects you back.

## Why 300?

The supply is fixed at 300 Genesis Kioke. No future minting function exists in the contract.

300 creates a meaningful per-barrel share of crafting revenue (1/300th of 1% of all presale ETH). Smaller supply means each barrel captures more. Larger supply dilutes per-barrel returns.

At 300 barrels and 10,000 ETH annual presale volume, each barrel earns 0.33 ETH passively. At 3,000 barrels, that drops to 0.033 ETH. The constraint is the point.

## Secondary Market Value

A Kioke Card appreciates based on three compounding factors:

1. **Koji accumulation.** Every factory launch adds 1 Koji to every barrel. A barrel held for 200 launches has 200 Koji. A newly minted barrel has zero. Koji cannot be purchased or transferred between barrels. Time in the protocol is the only way to accumulate it.

2. **Endorsement track record.** On-chain, anyone can verify which barrels endorsed which projects and when. A barrel with a history of backing successful projects carries signal value. That history transfers with the NFT.

3. **Revenue stream growth.** As Ponzu platform volume grows, crafting revenue per barrel increases. The revenue is tied to the barrel, not the wallet. Selling the NFT transfers the entire income stream.

A barrel bought at barrel #50 (0.20 ETH) that has accumulated 150 Koji and backed three successful projects has a fundamentally different value profile than the floor price. The asset compounds with use.
