## How It Works

Inflation-based farming destroyed projects in 2021. Mint new tokens, distribute to stakers, call it yield. The problem is obvious. You're diluting existing holders to pay new ones. Bots farm it, dump it, move on. Death by a thousand sells.

Ponzu's farm distributes rewards from actual economic activity. No new tokens are ever minted. The token contract has no mint function. Total supply is 1,000,000 at creation and stays at 1,000,000 forever.

**Reward sources:**

| Source | Type | Origin |
|--------|------|--------|
| Forfeited tokens from early presale claimers | Project tokens | Presale holders who claimed before vesting completed |
| 1% PonzuSwap trading fees | WETH | Every swap on PonzuSwap |
| LP penalties from early farm exits | LP tokens | Farmers who unstaked before 7 days |

Trading volume grows, ETH rewards grow. More people claim early, token rewards grow. Project health directly determines farm yield.

## Staking Mechanics

1. Get LP tokens by providing liquidity on PonzuSwap, or use ZapETH to convert ETH to LP in one transaction
2. Stake LP tokens in the Farm contract
3. Each stake mints a **Liquidity Card NFT** (ERC-721)
4. Rewards accumulate proportionally to your staked LP relative to total staked LP

**No top-ups.** Each `stakeNewCard()` call creates a new Liquidity Card with its own penalty timer and reward tracking.

### ZapETH

`zapETHToLP(tokenAddress, minLPTokens)` converts ETH into LP tokens in a single transaction. Splits your ETH, swaps half for project tokens, adds both sides as liquidity.

The `minLPTokens` parameter is your slippage protection. Set it to expected LP output minus your tolerance (e.g., 2%). If front-running or price movement causes the output to fall below your minimum, the transaction reverts and you keep your ETH.

## The 7-Day Conviction Window

Unstake before 7 days and you leave a portion of your LP behind.

| Days Staked | Penalty | LP Returned |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| 0 days | 100% | 0% |
| 1 day | 86% | 14% |
| 3 days | 57% | 43% |
| 3.5 days | 50% | 50% |
| 7+ days | 0% | 100% |

**Penalty formula:**
```
penalty = (7 - daysSinceStake) / 7 x 100%
```

At 0 days (same block as staking), the penalty is 100%. You get nothing back. Prevents flash-loan attacks where someone stakes, captures a reward distribution, and immediately unstakes.

**Where does the penalized LP go?**
- 50% added to remaining stakers' reward calculations. Their share of future rewards increases proportionally.
- 50% becomes protocol-owned liquidity. Permanently locked. Deepens the trading pool for everyone.

Impatient capital subsidizes the patient.

## Claim Types

### ETH Rewards (from swap fees via Distributor)
- Claimable anytime via `claimETH(cardId)`
- Repeatable. Claim as often as you want.
- No penalty on ETH claims
- Accumulated ETH tracked per Liquidity Card

### Token Rewards (from forfeited vesting tokens via Distributor)
- **One-time claim** per Liquidity Card
- Same diamond-hand principle as the presale: claim early and forfeit unvested tokens to remaining farm stakers
- Vested amount depends on when you claim relative to the reward epoch

**Unstake before claiming?** Unstaking burns your Liquidity Card. Unclaimed ETH rewards are claimed automatically during the unstake transaction. Unclaimed token rewards follow the one-time claim logic: you receive whatever has vested at the time of unstake, unvested portion goes to remaining stakers via the Distributor.

## Liquidity Card NFT

Each staking position is an ERC-721 NFT with independent state.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Minted on | Each `stakeNewCard()` call |
| Per user | Multiple cards allowed |
| Contains | Staked LP amount, stake timestamp, accumulated rewards |
| Transferable | Yes. Position, pending rewards, and penalty timer all transfer with the NFT |
| Burned on | `unstake()` |

**Multi-card strategy:** Stake at different times to manage different penalty clocks. Unstake Card #1 (no penalty after 7 days) while Card #2 continues earning. Claim ETH from any card independently. Each card's rewards tracked separately.

## Modeling Expected Returns

Farm APY depends on trading volume and total LP staked. Both change constantly. Here's the formula so you can model it yourself:

**ETH rewards per epoch (7 days):**
```
Your ETH = (yourStakedLP / totalStakedLP) x ethDistributedToFarm
```

Where `ethDistributedToFarm` depends on the Distributor's current farm allocation (20-40% of swap fee ETH, depending on claim progress).

**Example scenario:**
- Weekly swap volume: $500,000
- Swap fee: 1% = $5,000 in fees
- Distributor farm allocation (at 50% claimed): 30%
- ETH to farm: $1,500
- Your LP: 10% of total staked
- Your weekly ETH: $150

Volume up = rewards up. More LP staked = your share decreases. No inflation tricks. No vanishing APY from token emissions.

**Token rewards** are harder to predict. They depend on how many presale holders claim early and when. High early-claim activity = more forfeited tokens = more farm rewards.

## Risks

**Impermanent loss.** Standard AMM risk. You hold both the project token and ETH. If the token price moves significantly in either direction, your LP position can be worth less than holding the two assets separately. At large positions ($50K+), impermanent loss can exceed farming rewards during high-volatility periods. Model both sides.

**Low liquidity concentration.** If very little LP is staked, individual stakers capture disproportionate rewards. Feature for early stakers, but large reward batches can be captured by a small number of participants.

**Smart contract risk.** The farm contract is immutable post-deployment. No admin can upgrade, pause, or modify it. Bugs can't be patched. No one can rug you. Tradeoff accepted.
