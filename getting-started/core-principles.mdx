## Time is the only thing you can't counterfeit.

You can fake followers. Anyone with $500 can buy 50,000 accounts. You can fake volume, wash trading is so common it's barely worth mentioning. You can fake a chart. You can manufacture attention, pay KOLs to shill tokens they've never researched.

But you can't fake waiting. Either you held for 10 days or you didn't. Either you locked your capital or you bailed on day 1. Time is the one variable in hyper-financialized markets that remains incorruptible.

---

## Anti-extraction

Add a 10-day vesting period and the scam economics collapse. A scammer who can only run 3 scams per month instead of 500 per month isn't running a business anymore. The velocity that makes extraction profitable becomes impossible.

Capital that's locked can't be recycled. The same $10,000 can't pump and dump 500 different tokens in a month if it's stuck in a vesting contract. The weapon of the extractor, fast-moving capital that hits, extracts, and repeats, gets neutralized.

Time doesn't just slow down scams. It makes them economically irrational.

---

## Alignment through incentives

Diamond-hand vesting flips traditional vesting on its head.

Traditional vesting unlocks gradually over months. The problem: linear vesting creates thousands of micro-transactions. Claim a little today, a little tomorrow, sell continuously. Predictable, constant sell pressure. Cliffs only delay this death by a thousand cuts.

One claim. You pick your moment. Claim on Day 1, keep 10%, forfeit 90%. Claim on Day 10, keep 100% plus a pro-rata share of everything forfeited by everyone who claimed before you.

The patient holders' allocation grows as impatient holders exit.

---

## Permissionless exit

Exit any time, leave the rest. Ponzu doesn't trap capital.

- Refund from the presale: 90% ETH back
- Claim early from vesting: keep vested portion, leave the rest
- Unstake from the farm: penalty decreases daily over 7 days

The cost of leaving decreases with patience. No one is locked in. Everyone makes their choice. Each holder has different circumstances. Someone who needs liquidity early should be able to access it.

---

## Fee-based sustainability

Fixed 1,000,000 token supply. No mint function. All rewards come from real economic activity:

- 1% trading fees on PonzuSwap
- Forfeited tokens from early claimers
- 10% token penalty from refunds
- LP penalties from early farm exits

Zero inflation. Zero dilution. Every reward token was earned by someone else's impatience.
