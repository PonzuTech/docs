## What You Deploy

One transaction through PonzuRecipe creates your complete token ecosystem. Eight contracts, all immutable, all interconnected:

| Contract | What It Does |
|----------|-------------|
| **Token** | Your ERC-20. 1,000,000 fixed supply. No mint function exists |
| **Presale** | Sells 690,000 tokens (69%) at linear pricing |
| **Launcher** | Creates PonzuSwap liquidity when presale fills |
| **Distributor** | Routes swap fees and forfeited tokens to stakeholders |
| **Farm** | LP staking rewards for your community |
| **Ponzu Bottle** | ERC-721 NFT representing presale positions |
| **Liquidity Card** | ERC-721 NFT representing farm positions |
| **Project** | Registry and coordinator contract |

**Cost:** 0.005 ETH creation fee (paid to the protocol) + optional dev buy amount (your own presale purchase).

**Verification:** All eight contracts deploy from the PonzuRecipe factory contract. No proxy patterns. No upgrade functions. No admin keys. Read the source on Etherscan. Search for `selfdestruct`, `delegatecall`, `upgradeTo`. None exist.

## Configuration Choices

At deployment, you set these parameters. They are fixed permanently after:

| Parameter | Options | Notes |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Token Name | Any string | Your token's display name |
| Token Symbol | Any string | Your ticker (e.g., MYTKN) |
| Vesting Duration | 10 days (Yellow Bottle) or 10 weeks (Green Bottle) | See [diamond-hand vesting](/raise/diamond-hand-vesting) for tradeoffs |
| Target Raise | Sets the linear price curve | Minimum 3 ETH on mainnet. This determines your end price: `endPrice = targetRaise / 690,000` |
| Metadata | IPFS, Arweave, or HTTPS URI | Image, description, and social links |
| Dev Buy | Optional ETH amount | Your own initial presale purchase. Creates a Ponzu Bottle for you, subject to the same vesting as everyone else |

## Your Revenue: Complete Breakdown

Here's every ETH you earn, when you earn it, and where it comes from. Worked example uses a **50 ETH target raise**.

### During Presale
- **1% creator fee** on every ETH purchase
- Arrives in your wallet immediately with each buy
- On a 50 ETH raise: ~0.52 ETH total (1% of the ~52 ETH gross before the 4% fee split)

### At Launch
- **9% of the presale pool** goes directly to your wallet
- The presale pool is 96% of all ETH contributed (after the 4% fee was distributed during purchases)
- On a 50 ETH pool: **4.5 ETH** immediate runway

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)
- **15% of Distributor token allocation** from forfeited vesting tokens
- **15% of Distributor ETH allocation** from the 1% swap fee on every PonzuSwap trade
- These arrive continuously as trades happen and holders claim early

### Total from a 50 ETH Raise

| Source | Amount | When |
|--------|--------|------|
| Creator fee (1%) | ~0.52 ETH | During presale, per purchase |
| Treasury (9%) | 4.5 ETH | At launch, one transaction |
| Distributor (15% of tokens) | Variable, ongoing | Post-launch, from early claimers |
| Distributor (15% of ETH fees) | Variable, ongoing | Post-launch, from swap volume |
| **Guaranteed minimum** | **~5.02 ETH** | Before any trading occurs |

The ongoing Distributor revenue depends on trading volume and how many holders claim early. A project with active trading generates continuous ETH fees. A project where 60% of holders claim on Day 1 sends 15% of their forfeited tokens to your treasury.

## The Founder Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Configure your token and deploy through ponzu.app or the SDK. One transaction. All eight contracts are live and immutable.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build in Public">
    The presale runs in the background. Ship features. Share updates. Your 1% creator fee arrives with every purchase. No Zoom calls. No pitch decks. No term sheet negotiations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch Conviction Build">
    Linear pricing means early believers get better prices. As the presale fills, each purchase signals growing conviction at a higher price point. Your community forms around people who chose to buy, not people who received free allocations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Happens Automatically">
    When 690,000 tokens sell, anyone calls `triggerLaunch()`. PonzuSwap liquidity is created permanently. Your 9% ETH arrives in one transaction. Diamond-hand vesting begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn While Building">
    Post-launch, you receive ongoing revenue from the Distributor: 15% of swap fees and 15% of forfeited vesting tokens. Your community farms LP rewards. You build product. The contracts handle the rest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What the Contracts Handle vs. What You Do

| Task | You | Contracts |
|------|-----|-----------|
| Set token parameters | Once, at deployment | Enforced permanently |
| Run the presale | Share it with your community | Accepts purchases, mints Ponzu Bottles, tracks allocations |
| Process refunds | Nothing | Handles 90% refund, 10% penalty redistribution |
| Create liquidity | Nothing | Pairs tokens + ETH on PonzuSwap automatically |
| Lock LP | Nothing | LP tokens sit in a contract with no withdrawal function |
| Vest tokens | Nothing | Linear vesting, one-time claim, forfeit redistribution |
| Distribute fees | Nothing | Routes 1% swap fees to all stakeholders dynamically |
| Manage farming | Nothing | LP staking, reward distribution, penalty enforcement |
| Receive treasury | Claim it | 9% ETH sent to your wallet at launch |

After deployment, every operational step is handled by immutable contracts. Your job is to build the product that makes the token worth holding.

## What If the Presale Stalls?

There is no time limit on the presale. If your project sits at 30% filled for months, here's what's true:

- **You keep all creator fees earned so far.** The 1% from every purchase is already in your wallet.
- **Buyers can refund.** They get 90% of their ETH back. The 10% token penalty stays in the pool and benefits remaining holders.
- **You cannot cancel the presale.** The contracts are immutable. The presale will remain open until it fills.
- **You can keep building.** Ship features. Share updates. A slow presale that eventually fills because the product proved itself is better than a fast sellout with nothing behind it.

A presale that fills over 6 months at increasingly higher prices, because the founder shipped real product along the way, creates a healthier community than one that fills in 6 minutes on hype alone.

## Technical: Using the SDK

Deploy through the web app at ponzu.app, or programmatically with the SDK:

```typescript
import { deploy } from '@ponzu_app/sdk'
import { parseEther } from 'viem'

const result = await deploy(
  {
    owner:          account.address,    // Your wallet, receives treasury + creator fees
    tokenName:      'My Token',
    tokenSymbol:    'MYTKN',
    metadata:       'ipfs://Qm...',     // Project description + socials
    imageURI:       'ipfs://Qm...',     // Token image
    targetEthRaise: parseEther('5'),    // Minimum 3 ETH on mainnet
  },
  wallet,       // viem WalletClient
  client,       // viem PublicClient
  'mainnet',    // 'mainnet' or 'sepolia'
)

// All eight contract addresses are returned:
// result.addresses.token
// result.addresses.presale
// result.addresses.launcher
// result.addresses.distributor
// result.addresses.farm
// result.addresses.ponzuBottle
// result.addresses.liquidityCard
// result.addresses.project
```

The `deploy()` function sends a single transaction to PonzuRecipe. All contracts are created atomically. If any part fails, the entire deployment reverts.

Full SDK reference: [SDK on NPM](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@ponzu_app/sdk)
