Every mechanism in Ponzu is designed with the investor in mind. The goal is founder-investor alignment, not through coercion, but through economics. Nothing is locked. You can claim your tokens anytime, get a refund anytime, sell your position anytime. But every exit path is designed so that leaving doesn't create sell pressure on the token. Refunds return ETH at cost, not a market dump. Positions are NFTs you sell on secondary, not tokens you dump on the DEX. You can claim your ETH rewards whenever you want. But if you want to claim your tokens to sell them, you only get that right once, not death by a thousand cuts. The result is a token economy where the most profitable strategy is also the most aligned one.

- **Lifetime ETH rewards.** 1% of every swap on PonzuSwap flows through the Distributor. Your Ponzu Bottle earns ETH as long as the project trades. Claim ETH anytime, as often as you want, without affecting your token position. Even after vesting, leave your tokens unclaimed and keep earning indefinitely.

## How it works
### Linear pricing

Price starts at 10% of the target market cap and increases linearly. A project targeting \$1M starts at \$100K. The curve is set at deployment and cannot be changed. No hidden rounds. No OTC deals. No insider discounts.

Your presale allocation is an NFT — a **Ponzu Bottle** (ERC-721) that holds your entire position: token allocation, ETH contributed, vesting status, accumulated ETH rewards.


###  90% Refunds 
Before launch, you can claim a 90% refund on what you paid, anytime. The thinking shifts from "is this a rug?" to "is this team worth backing?" If the presale has reached \$400K and you bought in at \$100K, you have a choice: take the 90% ETH refund, or sell the Ponzu Bottle on secondary. New owner inherits everything — token claim rights, refund option, accumulated ETH rewards. There is liquidity, but no sell pressure, no dumping. Only refunds. Rest until launch.

### Diamond-hand vesting

Diamond-hand vesting flips traditional vesting on its head.
Traditional vesting unlocks gradually over months. The problem: linear vesting creates thousands of micro-transactions. Claim a little today, a little tomorrow, sell continuously. Predictable, constant sell pressure. Cliffs only delay this death by a thousand cuts. For liquid tokens, they also introduce sell pressure from investors hedging their exposure since they cant exit by adding short positions.

After launch, you can claim anytime. But you can only claim once.

If the token trades at 20x your entry price on Day 1, an early claim gives you 10%, still a 2x overnight, the rest gets redistributed. Waiting to the end gives you 100%, plus a share of the redistributed tokens as a bonus.

A 10-day vest means the project has 10 days to prove itself. A 10-month vest gives the token more time to build traction. For the first time, tokens now have time baked into the contract. 


### ETH rewards

Your Ponzu Bottle accumulates ETH from the Distributor while vesting and beyond. 1% of every swap on PonzuSwap flows through. Claim ETH anytime, as often as you want, without affecting your token position. There is no deadline to claim.
