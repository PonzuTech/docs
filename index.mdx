## Exit early, but leave the rest.

We've watched token launches devolve into casinos. Insiders get cheap tokens, dump on the community, and disappear. Bots front-run every trade. What you need is time. Time to build traction. Conviction over time.

Ponzu is a token launcher on Ethereum that adds time throughout the token lifecycle thanks to Diamond hand vesting. You can only claim once, if you claim early, you unvested tokens get redistributed to the patient.

What this means is that there just isnt enough claimable tokens to dump at launch. 

For 10 day diamond hand vesting, there would only be 0.4% available to claim. Claim on day one, keep 10%, leave the rest. Wait ten days, get 100% plus a share of redistributed totkens. 

The weak hands subsidize the diamond hands.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Presale">
    Linear pricing. Price starts at a tenth of your target market cap and rises with each buy. The market validates your pricing through participation, not arbitrage. Each position is a transferable Ponzu Bottle NFT. 690,000 tokens available. 90% ETH refund before launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Presale fills, anyone triggers launch. 310,000 tokens + raised ETH become permanent liquidity on PonzuSwap. LP tokens sit in a contract with no withdrawal function. The dev is dead the moment that button is pressed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diamond-Hand Vesting">
    One claim. That's it. Claim early, leave the rest for the patient. Wait the full vest, get your allocation plus a pro-rata bonus from everyone who left early. No one is locked in. Everyone makes their choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Farming">
    Stake LP, earn trading fees and redistributed tokens. Fixed 1,000,000 supply. No mint function. Every reward came from real economic activity. Impatient capital subsidizes the patient. Same principle, extended.
  </Step>
</Steps>
