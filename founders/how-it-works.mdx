## The Lifecycle

Every token launched through Ponzu follows the same path. No exceptions. No special deals. No admin overrides.

```
CRAFT → PRESALE → LAUNCH → VEST → FARM
```

---

## Step 1: Craft

A founder deploys their token through the PonzuRecipe factory. One transaction creates token, presale, launcher, distributor, farm, and NFT contracts.

**Cost:** 0.005 ETH creation fee + optional dev buy.

**What gets set at creation:**
- Token name and symbol
- Pricing curve (start price to end price, linear)
- Vesting duration (10 days or 10 weeks)
- Metadata (image, description, socials)

After this transaction, the founder has no admin keys. Every parameter is fixed.

**How to verify:** The `PonzuCrafted` event on PonzuRecipe ([`0x1155484c5fE614538d83c444f9a6dB662E6a7153`](https://etherscan.io/address/0x1155484c5fE614538d83c444f9a6dB662E6a7153)) emits all eight contract addresses. Pull the event, read each contract on Etherscan, confirm there are no owner functions or upgrade proxies.

## Step 2: Presale

The presale opens immediately. Users send ETH and receive tokens at a linearly rising price.

**Token allocation:** 690,000 tokens (69% of supply) available in presale.

**Price curve:** The founder sets an end price (target market cap). Start price is 10% of that. Price increases linearly with each token sold.

```
startPrice = endPrice / 10
currentPrice = startPrice + (endPrice - startPrice) * (tokensSold / 690,000)
```

**Example with real numbers:** If a founder sets endPrice to 100 Gwei per token (targeting a ~100 ETH raise), the startPrice is 10 Gwei. First buyer pays ~10 Gwei/token. Last buyer pays ~100 Gwei/token. The average price across the entire presale is ~55 Gwei/token.

**What happens on each purchase:**
1. User sends ETH
2. 4% fee deducted and split: 1% creator, 1% protocol, 1% Kioke, 1% referrer
3. 96% held in contract until launch
4. Ponzu Bottle NFT minted (one per user per project)
5. Subsequent purchases increase the same NFT's allocation

**Refund option:** Before launch, any buyer can refund for 90% of their ETH. The 10% token penalty stays in the pool and redistributes pro-rata to remaining holders.

**Worst case math:** You buy 1 ETH into the presale. 0.04 ETH goes to fees immediately. You decide the project is bad and refund. You get back 90% of your remaining token value in ETH. Your total loss is the 4% fee + 10% token penalty. Not 100%.

<Info>
Your Ponzu Bottle NFT is transferable. Selling the NFT transfers your entire presale position, including the right to claim tokens or request a refund.
</Info>

## Step 3: Launch

When all 690,000 presale tokens are sold, launch becomes available. Anyone can trigger it (permissionless).

**What happens:**
1. 310,000 tokens + 91% of all raised ETH create a trading pair on PonzuSwap
2. LP tokens are sent to the Presale contract and locked permanently (no function exists to withdraw them)
3. 9% of ETH goes to the project treasury as immediate runway
4. Vesting clock starts
5. Trading opens on PonzuSwap

**How to verify LP is locked:** After launch, check the PonzuSwap pair contract. The LP tokens sit in the Presale contract address. Read the Presale contract source. There is no `withdrawLP`, `removeLiquidity`, or `transferLP` function. The LP tokens are in the contract permanently because no code path exists to move them.

**Anti-sniper protection:** PonzuSwap charges 20% on swaps during the first hour, decaying linearly to 1%. A sniper buying at launch and selling for a 10% gain actually loses 10% net. By minute 30, the fee is ~10.5%. By minute 60, it's 1%. Normal trading begins after the first hour.

## Step 4: Diamond-Hand Vesting

After launch, presale holders have 10 days (or 10 weeks) to claim their tokens. They can claim at any time, but only once.

| Claim Day | You Receive | Forfeited to Community |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Day 1 | 10% | 90% |
| Day 3 | 30% | 70% |
| Day 5 | 50% | 50% |
| Day 10 | 100% + bonus | 0% |

**The bonus:** Patient holders who wait the full vesting period receive their full allocation plus a pro-rata share of everything forfeited by early claimers.

**Worked example:** 10 holders each own 10,000 tokens (100,000 total in presale). 5 holders claim on Day 1, each keeping 1,000 tokens and forfeiting 9,000 tokens. The 45,000 forfeited tokens go to the Distributor, which routes 40% (18,000) back to the 5 remaining presale holders. Each patient holder's allocation grows from 10,000 to 13,600 tokens. That's a 36% bonus for waiting.

**One claim only.** You choose your moment. The Ponzu Bottle is burned after claiming. No partial exits. No "vest and leave" optimization.

Forfeited tokens flow to the Distributor, which routes them to remaining presale holders and farm stakers.

## Step 5: Farm

After launch, LP staking is available. Users provide liquidity on PonzuSwap and stake LP tokens to earn rewards.

**Reward sources (non-inflationary):**
- Forfeited tokens from early claimers
- 1% swap fees from PonzuSwap trading
- LP penalties from early farm exits

**Each stake mints a Liquidity Card NFT.** Positions are transferable.

**7-day minimum staking period:**
- Unstake before 7 days = linear penalty (100% at day 0, 0% at day 7)
- 50% of penalty goes to remaining stakers
- 50% goes to protocol-owned liquidity

**Claim types:**
- ETH rewards: claimable anytime, repeatable
- Token rewards: one-time claim per card (same diamond-hand principle)

## Fee Structure Summary

All fees in one place:

| Fee | Amount | When | Where it goes |
|-----|--------|------|---------------|
| Presale purchase | 4% of ETH | Every buy | 1% creator, 1% protocol, 1% Kioke, 1% referrer |
| Refund penalty | 10% of tokens | Before launch | Pro-rata to remaining presale holders |
| Swap fee (launch hour) | 20% decaying to 1% | First 60 minutes | Distributor |
| Swap fee (normal) | 1% | Every trade after hour 1 | Distributor |
| Farm early exit | Linear, 100% to 0% over 7 days | Unstaking before 7 days | 50% stakers, 50% protocol |

## The Distributor

All fees and forfeited tokens flow through the Distributor. Here's how it splits:

**Token distribution:**

| Recipient | Share | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Presale holders | 40% → 0% | Decreases as presale holders claim |
| Farm stakers | 40% → 80% | Increases as presale empties |
| Project treasury | 15% | Fixed |
| Kioke holders | 5% | Fixed |

**ETH distribution:**

| Recipient | Share | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Protocol | 40% | Fixed |
| Presale holders | 20% → 0% | Decreases as presale holders claim |
| Farm stakers | 20% → 40% | Increases as presale empties |
| Project treasury | 15% | Fixed |
| Kioke holders | 5% | Fixed |

The shift from presale to farm happens automatically as presale holders claim their tokens. More early claims = more rewards flowing to the farm. This is why patient farm stakers benefit from impatient presale holders.

## The Complete Flow

```
Community buys Presale → Ponzu Bottle NFT minted
                              ↓
Presale sells out → Launcher creates PonzuSwap pair
                  → LP tokens locked permanently
                  → 9% ETH to project treasury
                              ↓
Vesting begins → Claim early (forfeit unvested → community)
               → Claim late (get everything + bonus)
                              ↓
Trading on PonzuSwap → 1% swap fees → Distributor
                              ↓
Distributor splits → Tokens: 40% presale, 40% farm, 15% project, 5% Kioke
                   → ETH: 20% presale, 20% farm, 15% project, 5% Kioke, 40% protocol
                              ↓
Farm stakers earn → Token rewards + WETH from fees
                 → 7-day minimum, early exit penalty
```

Every position is an NFT. Ponzu Bottle for presale. Liquidity Card for farming. Kioke Card for launch rights. All transferable. All composable.

## Quick DD Checklist

When evaluating a specific Ponzu project, check these:

| Check | What to look for | Contract call |
|-------|------------------|---------------|
| Supply | Should be exactly 1,000,000 | `totalSupply()` on Token |
| Presale progress | How much has sold, how close to launch | `tokensAvailable()` on Presale |
| Price check | Current price vs. start/end to gauge entry timing | `currentPrice()` on Presale |
| Vesting duration | 10 days vs. 10 weeks changes your hold timeline | `vestingDuration` on Presale |
| Refund activity | How many people left. Signal for sentiment. | `Refunded` events on Presale |
| LP status (post-launch) | Confirm LP tokens are in Presale contract, no withdrawal function | Check pair LP token `balanceOf(presaleAddress)` |
| Mint function | Should not exist | Read Token source on Etherscan |
