## A token factory that adds time.

We launch roughly a million tokens per month. Serial deployers drain 80% of all liquidity within 24 hours. A small cartel of 12 wallet clusters engineers nearly 20% of token creations while extracting millions. The business model is velocity: deploy, pump, drain, repeat.

Ponzu deploys eight contracts in a single transaction. Presale, DEX pool, vesting, farming, distributor, NFT positions. All immutable. No admin keys. No upgrade proxies.

Every mechanism follows one principle: **exit early, but leave the rest.**

---

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Presale">
    Linear pricing. Price starts at a tenth of your target and rises with each buy. Each position is a transferable Ponzu Bottle NFT. 690,000 tokens available (69% of supply). 90% ETH refund before launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Presale fills, anyone triggers launch. 310,000 tokens + raised ETH become permanent liquidity on PonzuSwap. LP tokens sit in a contract with no withdrawal function. The dev is dead the moment that button is pressed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diamond-Hand Vesting">
    One claim. That's it. Claim early, leave the rest for the patient. Wait the full vest, get your allocation plus a pro-rata bonus from everyone who left early. No one is locked in. Everyone makes their choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Farming">
    Stake LP, earn trading fees and redistributed tokens. Fixed 1,000,000 supply. No mint function. Every reward came from real economic activity. Impatient capital subsidizes the patient. Same principle, extended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The numbers

## Eight contracts, one transaction

| Contract | What it does |
|----------|-------------|
| **Token** | ERC-20 with 1,000,000 fixed supply. No mint function. |
| **Presale** | Purchases, refunds, vesting, claims. 690,000 tokens. |
| **Launcher** | Creates DEX liquidity at graduation. 310,000 tokens. |
| **Distributor** | Routes all fees dynamically between presale holders, farmers, project, and Kioke. |
| **Farm** | LP staking for non-inflationary rewards. |
| **Ponzu Bottle** | ERC-721 NFT representing a presale position. Transferable. |
| **Liquidity Card** | ERC-721 NFT representing a farm staking position. Transferable. |
| **Project** | Registry and access control coordinator. |

After deployment, all parameters are fixed. No upgrade proxies. No admin keys. No migration steps.

## Verify it yourself

Every claim above is on-chain.

| Claim | How to verify |
|-------|---------------|
| Fixed 1M supply | Call `totalSupply()` on the Token contract |
| No mint function | Read the Token contract source on Etherscan. No `mint()` exists. |
| 69/31 split | Check `balanceOf(presaleAddress)` and `balanceOf(launcherAddress)` at deployment |
| No admin keys | No `owner`, `admin`, or `onlyOwner` modifiers in any contract after init |
| LP permanently locked | The Presale contract holds LP tokens. No `withdraw` or `transfer` function exists for them. |
| Fee splits | Read the Distributor. Hardcoded percentages, no setter functions. |
