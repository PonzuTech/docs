## Launch Trigger

When all 690,000 presale tokens are sold, `triggerLaunch()` becomes callable. Anyone can call it. No admin action required.

```
require(tokensSold == 690000 && launched == false)
```

You don't need to be online. Any wallet can trigger the launch once the presale fills.

## What Happens

The launch sequence executes atomically in a single transaction:

<Steps>
  <Step title="ETH Split">
    The presale pool (96% of all ETH contributed, after the 4% fee was already distributed during purchases) splits:
    - **91%** to the Launcher contract for liquidity creation
    - **9%** directly to the project creator's wallet

    **Example:** Presale pool holds 100 ETH. 91 ETH goes to liquidity. 9 ETH goes to the founder. The founder also already received ~1.04 ETH in creator fees during the presale (1% of ~104 ETH gross).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Liquidity Creation">
    The Launcher pairs 310,000 tokens (31% of total supply, reserved at deployment) with the 91% ETH and creates a TKN/WETH trading pair on PonzuSwap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permanent LP Lock">
    LP tokens are sent to the Presale contract. There is **no `withdrawLP()` function**. No admin function. No timelock. No multisig override. The LP tokens sit in a contract with no code path to remove them.

    Read the Presale contract source on Etherscan. Search for any function that transfers LP tokens out. There isn't one. The dev is dead the moment that button is pressed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vesting Begins">
    `launchTime` is set on-chain. Diamond-hand vesting starts from this block. Presale holders now have 10 days (Yellow Bottle) or 10 weeks (Green Bottle) to claim their tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trading Opens">
    The token is immediately tradeable on PonzuSwap. Anti-sniper fee protection is active for the first hour.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Anti-Sniper Fee Decay

Swap fees start at 20% and decay linearly to 1% over the first 60 minutes:

| Time After Launch | Swap Fee | Round-Trip Cost (buy + sell) |
|-------------------|----------|------------------------------|
| 0 minutes | 20% | ~36% loss |
| 15 minutes | 15.25% | ~28% loss |
| 30 minutes | 10.5% | ~20% loss |
| 45 minutes | 5.75% | ~11% loss |
| 60 minutes | 1% (permanent) | ~2% loss |

A sniper buying at launch pays 20% on the buy (gets 80% of expected tokens). Selling immediately costs another 20%. Net result: they keep ~64% of their original ETH. A token would need to pump 56% in the same block just to break even.

The fees don't disappear. They flow to the Distributor, which routes them to presale holders, farm stakers, and the project treasury. Snipers who try anyway end up subsidizing patient holders. Impatient capital exits, leaving behind tokens for the patient.

**On-chain:** Fee decay is calculated from `launchTime` using `getCurrentFee()`. After 60 minutes, the fee is permanently 1%.

## Price Discovery

Initial DEX price is the ratio of assets deposited into the PonzuSwap pool:

```
initialPrice = (91% of presale pool ETH) / 310,000 tokens
```

**Example:** Presale pool holds 100 ETH at launch. 91 ETH + 310,000 tokens go to the pool. Initial price = 91 / 310,000 = 0.000294 ETH per token.

The last presale buyer paid roughly `endPrice` per token. The initial DEX price will be close but not identical, because 31% of supply (the liquidity tokens) were never sold in the presale. From here, the market determines price. The pool has permanent liquidity.
