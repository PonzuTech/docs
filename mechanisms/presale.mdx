## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Presale">
    Linear pricing. Price starts at a tenth of your target and rises with each buy. Each position is a transferable Ponzu Bottle NFT. 690,000 tokens available (69% of supply). 90% ETH refund before launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Presale fills, token launches. 310,000 tokens + raised ETH become liquidity on PonzuSwap. LP tokens sit in a contract with no withdrawal function. The dev is dead the moment that button is pressed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diamond-Hand Vesting">
    One claim. That's it. Claim early, leave the rest for the patient. Wait the full vest, get your allocation plus a pro-rata bonus from everyone who left early. No one is locked in. Everyone makes their choice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Farming">
    Stake LP, earn trading fees and redistributed tokens. Fixed 1,000,000 supply. No mint function. Every reward came from real economic activity. Impatient capital subsidizes the patient. Same principle, extended.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Linear Pricing

The founder sets a target raise. Price starts at one-tenth of the final price and increases at a constant rate as tokens sell.

```
startPrice = endPrice / 10
currentPrice = startPrice + (endPrice - startPrice) * (tokensSold / 690,000)
```

**Worked example:** 10 ETH target raise.

| Tokens Sold | Price per Token | Cumulative ETH Spent |
|-------------|----------------|---------------------|
| 0 (first buyer) | ~0.0000014 ETH | 0 ETH |
| 172,500 (25%) | ~0.0000050 ETH | ~0.55 ETH |
| 345,000 (50%) | ~0.0000086 ETH | ~2.20 ETH |
| 517,500 (75%) | ~0.0000122 ETH | ~4.95 ETH |
| 690,000 (100%) | ~0.0000158 ETH | ~10 ETH |

First buyer pays 10x less than the last buyer. Early conviction gets a better price.

## Presale Purchase Fee (4%)

Every presale purchase has a 4% fee on ETH input, distributed immediately:

| Recipient | Share |
|-----------|-------|
| Creator (project deployer) | 1% |
| Ponzu protocol | 1% |
| Kioke holders | 1% |
| Order referrer | 1% |

This fee is deducted from your ETH before it enters the presale pool. On a 1 ETH purchase, 0.04 ETH goes to fees and 0.96 ETH goes toward your token allocation.

---

## Refunds

Before launch, any buyer can refund to exit. You get 90% of your ETH back, no questions.

The remaining 10% is effectively sold. Tokens get redistributed and the ETH remains, giving everyone a boost in presale allocation. The weak hands subsidize the diamond hands.

## Presale Completion

The presale completes when all 690,000 tokens are sold. No time limit. Projects can sell out in hours or months. Ship features while the presale fills.
