## The Genesis Sale

300 Genesis Kioke barrels. Each is an ERC-721 NFT that earns revenue from every project launched on Ponzu through two streams: passive crafting fees (1% of all presale ETH) and active fermenting rewards (5% of endorsed project Distributor output).

The first barrel holders shape what gets launched on Ponzu. Your Koji endorsements are the signal layer.

## Pricing

Linear pricing. Each sale increases the price by a fixed increment. The same pricing mechanism Ponzu uses for token presales.

**Starting price:** 0.1 ETH
**Price increment:** 0.002 ETH per barrel sold

```

Barrel #150: 0.40 ETH
```

No countdown timer. No Dutch auction. No hidden allocation.

If all 300 barrels sell, total ETH raised is approximately **120 ETH** (average price ~0.40 ETH across the curve).

Early buyers pay less and own the same asset. The only advantage is timing.

The core question: at what platform volume does a barrel pay for itself from crafting revenue alone?

**Crafting revenue per barrel = (Annual presale volume x 1%) / 300**

| Your Purchase Price | Annual Presale Volume to Break Even (Crafting Only) |

| 0.10 ETH (Barrel #1) | 3,000 ETH |

## Investment Framing

A Kioke Card sits somewhere between an exchange token and a venture fund allocation:

| Comparison | Kioke Card |
|------------|-----------|
| **Exchange token** (BNB, FTT model) | Similar: earns from platform activity. Different: fixed 300 supply, no dilution, fully on-chain, no centralized operator risk |
| **LP position** | Similar: earns fees from trading. Different: earns from ALL projects, not a single pair. No impermanent loss |
| **Validator node** | Similar: infrastructure-level revenue. Different: no hardware, no uptime requirements, fully passive baseline |
| **VC fund LP** | Similar: portfolio-level exposure across multiple projects. Different: no management fee, no GP carry, fully liquid position |

The closest analog: **a fixed seat on a permissionless exchange that earns a cut of every listing and every trade, where your curation decisions add an active management layer on top.**

## What Happens If the Sale Doesn't Fill

All 300 barrels exist in the contract. Unsold barrels remain available at their price point on the curve. There is no deadline and no minimum fill threshold.

If 150 barrels sell and 150 remain, the protocol operates with 150 active barrels. Crafting revenue still distributes across all 300, but unsold barrels accumulate unclaimed revenue and Koji that future buyers inherit when they purchase.

A buyer who purchases barrel #200 after 100 projects have launched gets 100 accumulated Koji with the barrel. They also get backdated crafting revenue that accumulated while the barrel was unsold. Late buyers pay a higher price but inherit accrued value.
